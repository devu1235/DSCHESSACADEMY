Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our chess programs

We specialize in teaching children from ages 4 to 16. Our curriculum is tailored to different age groups with appropriate teaching methods for each developmental stage.

All you need is a computer or tablet with internet connection. We'll provide digital chess boards for practice. For offline practice, any standard chess set will work.

Flexible morning, afternoon, and weekend slots are available. Time zones for international students (India, USA, UK) are considered to provide a smooth scheduling experience.

We understand that unexpected situations can come up. In case your child misses a class, we offer up to 2 makeup classes per month to ensure they stay on track with their learning.

Most students show noticeable improvement in 8-12 weeks with regular practice. Tournament-ready skills typically develop in 6-12 months depending on the student's dedication.
